About Us

Over the years, the Bangladesh Olympiad in Informatics (BdOI) has seen remarkable growth, driven by a culture of collaboration and mentorship. One of the key reasons for this success is the support from seniors who pass on their knowledge and experiences to juniors.

We aim to continue this tradition, by introducing "Thonk & Code". We want to ensure that even beginners have the support to start in the world of Competitive Programming and Informatics Olympiads and learn the thought process needed for solving CP problems. Although this program is aimed for getting talented secondary school and college students in BdOI, others including university students are also welcomed.
